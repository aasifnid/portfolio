Cuemath · 2021–22 · Game Design

Curriculum Games

Educational games designed for Cuemath's trial and demo classes — minimal screens, content-driven, built to give students a taste of the learning experience before enrolment.

The context

These games sit at the beginning of my journey at Cuemath — before I transitioned into product design. The brief was always minimal: a concept, a learning goal, and a student age group. From there, ideation, wireframes, review, and final screens.

The work was content-first. The game mechanics existed to make practice feel less like practice. That thinking — engagement as a product requirement, not a feature — stayed with me into everything I designed after.
